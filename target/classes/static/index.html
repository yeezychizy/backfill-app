Backfill for Teachers

Backfill is a school management platform that helps you manage your teacher backfill process, notifications, and HR tools.

What you can do with Backfill

Manage your teacher backfill process

Use Backfill to manage your teacher backfill process. You can view and edit the status of your backfill requests, see a list of all backfill requests, and more.

Set up notifications for your teachers

Set up notifications for your teachers. You can configure notifications to be sent to your teachers when specific events occur, such as when they have a new class or when they are assigned to a class with a new student.

Manage your teacher backfill process

Access HR tools. You can use Backfill's HR tools to manage your teachers' profiles, view their contracts, and more.
